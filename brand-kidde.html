BRAND PROFILE

About Kidde

Founded by Walter Kidde in 1917, Kidde is one of the world's largest manufacturers of fire extinguishers, smoke alarms and carbon monoxide alarms. The company produced the first portable carbon dioxide fire extinguisher in 1924 and the first built-in industrial extinguishing system the following year, and today remains a trusted name in portable and fixed fire suppression equipment for homes and businesses.
