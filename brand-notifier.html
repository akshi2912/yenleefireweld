BRAND PROFILE

About Notifier

NOTIFIER, a Honeywell brand, has been in a leadership position in the fire alarm industry for over 70 years and is recognised as the world's largest manufacturer of engineered fire alarm systems. Its addressable control panels, intelligent detectors and voice evacuation solutions protect complex facilities such as airports, hospitals, campuses and data centres, with a global network of certified distributors providing local support and service.
