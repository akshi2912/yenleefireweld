Fire Hydrant Systems

Installation and maintenance of hydrants, hose reels, landing valves and fire department connections for fixed water-based fire protection.

We install and maintain the fixed hydrant infrastructure that gives firefighting teams reliable access to water under pressure, from landing valves to fire department connections.

We install and maintain complete hydrant systems including external hydrants, internal hose reels, landing valves and fire department connections, ensuring adequate flow and pressure across your site. Systems are tested and certified to confirm they perform correctly when a fire brigade needs to connect.

This service typically covers:

  • Hydrant coverage assessment
  • Landing valve and hose reel installation
  • Fire department connection installation
  • System pressure testing
01

Water Supply Study

We assess your water supply, pressure and hydrant coverage requirements.

02

Installation

We install hydrants, landing valves, hose reels and fire department connections.

03

Pressure Testing

We test the system to confirm adequate pressure and flow at every hydrant point.

04

Maintenance Plan
