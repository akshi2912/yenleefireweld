This station combines a dual-head eyewash bowl with an overhead deluge shower, both hands-free activated for immediate use during a chemical exposure incident. Freeze-protection and flow-control options are available for outdoor or unheated installations, and the unit is designed for the 15-minute continuous flush recommended by safety standards.
