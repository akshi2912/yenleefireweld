Foam Equipment

Supply, installation and commissioning of branchpipes, inductors, foam chambers, pourers and portable foam monitors for fixed and semi-fixed foam systems.

From bladder tanks to inductors and pourers, we design, supply and install the hardware that gets your foam concentrate onto the fire fast and evenly, then commission and test the complete system before hand-over.

From inline inductors that proportion foam concentrate accurately, to foam chambers on storage tanks and portable monitors for mobile response, we supply and install the hardware that turns foam concentrate into an effective firefighting agent. Every installation is sized and positioned according to your tank, hazard and flow-rate requirements.

This service typically covers:

  • Application-specific equipment recommendation
  • Professional installation by trained technicians
  • Full system commissioning and function testing
  • Compatibility check with existing pipework and pumps
01

Site Assessment

We assess your tank, bund or process area to determine the right foam application method and equipment layout.

02

Equipment Selection & Supply

We supply the matching branchpipes, inductors, chambers or pourers for your application.

03

Installation

Our technicians install and integrate the equipment into your fire protection system.

04

Commissioning & Testing

We commission and function-test the system to confirm correct foam application before hand-over.
