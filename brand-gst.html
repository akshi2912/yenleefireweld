BRAND PROFILE

About GST

Established in 1993, GST (Gulf Security Technology) is a leading fire alarm and life-safety brand in China and across Asia, and is now part of Carrier's Kidde Global Solutions. GST designs, manufactures and distributes a complete range of fire alarm control panels, detectors and fire alarm network products, holding certifications including CCC, UL, LPCB and CE, with products sold in more than 80 countries.
