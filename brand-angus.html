About Angus

Angus Fire is a UK-based global leader in firefighting technology, with a history of manufacturing fire protection products stretching back more than 220 years. The company supplies customers in over 100 countries, combining expertise in rubber and textile technology, speciality foam chemistry and engineering design to produce fire hose, foam concentrates and firefighting equipment used by municipal and industrial fire services worldwide.
