Designed for technical rope rescue teams, this kit pairs static/low-stretch kernmantle rope with a rated descent-control device, prusik cord for backup, and a full set of locking carabiners and rigging plates. It is configured for single or two-person controlled descent, edge transitions and casualty lowering from height.

Main RopeLow-stretch kernmantle, life-safety rated
DescenderRated descent-control / figure-8 device
CarabinersAuto-locking, NFPA/EN rated (set of 6)
Prusik CordBackup friction hitch cord, 2 lengths
Anchor StrapsAdjustable, rated for rescue anchoring
Edge ProtectionRope pad / edge roller included
StandardNFPA 1983 & EN 341 aligned hardware
MaintenanceRope log & annual inspection recommended
  • Rated for single or two-person controlled descent
  • Includes prusik backup for redundant fall protection
  • Edge protection to prevent rope abrasion on structures
  • Compact rope bag for fast deployment and clean stowage
  • Compatible with standard rescue harness attachment points
  • Supplied with a rope-use log for lifecycle tracking
