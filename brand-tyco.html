BRAND PROFILE

About Tyco

Tyco, now part of Johnson Controls, invented the modern fire sprinkler in 1881 and continues to offer one of the broadest ranges of water-based fire suppression products in the industry. Its portfolio spans automatic sprinklers, valves, piping and firefighting equipment, supplied alongside sister brands such as Ansul and Chemguard to customers in more than 190 countries worldwide.
