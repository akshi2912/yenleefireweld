Foam Concentrates

Supply of AFFF, AR-AFFF, Protein and Fluorine-Free foam concentrates, matched to your risk profile and backed by proper handling, storage and top-up support.

We help you select the right foam concentrate for your fire risk — hydrocarbon, polar solvent or environmentally sensitive sites — then handle delivery, safe storage guidance and stock rotation so your foam system is always ready to perform when it matters.

Our foam concentrates are selected to match the specific hazard class of your site — from AFFF for fast knockdown on hydrocarbon fires to fluorine-free formulations for sites moving away from PFAS-based chemistry. We help you choose the right concentrate, expansion ratio and application rate, then supply it in drums, totes or bulk tanks with full batch documentation and safety data sheets.

This service typically covers:

  • Site-specific foam concentrate recommendation
  • Certified, quality-checked stock from trusted manufacturers
  • Guidance on safe storage and shelf-life management
  • Scheduled top-up and replacement support
01

Risk & Compliance Review

We review your fire risk, tank/bund layout and any regulatory requirements (SCDF, SS EN, ICAO) to identify the right foam type and concentration.

02

Product Selection & Supply

We recommend and supply the matching concentrate — AFFF, AR-AFFF, Protein or Fluorine-Free — from certified stock.

03

Delivery & Storage Guidance

We deliver to site and advise on correct storage conditions, shelf-life management and safe handling.

04

Top-Up & Replacement Plan

We set up a stock rotation and replenishment schedule so concentrate levels and quality are always maintained.

Faster Fire Knockdown

Foam blankets suppress flammable-liquid fires quickly by cutting off oxygen and vapour release.

Reduced Re-ignition Risk

A stable foam blanket keeps fuel surfaces sealed, lowering the chance of re-ignition.

Environmentally Responsible Options

Fluorine-free and AR-AFFF choices help sites meet evolving environmental requirements.
