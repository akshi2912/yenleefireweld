Emergency Exit Lights

Supply and installation of LED exit signs, emergency lights and escape route lighting systems for commercial buildings.

We plan and install exit signage and emergency lighting along your building's escape routes, so occupants can find their way out safely during a power failure or emergency.

We supply and install LED emergency exit signs, escape route lighting and emergency lighting systems that activate automatically during a power failure, guiding occupants safely to exits. Battery backup units are tested regularly to confirm reliable operation when mains power is lost.

This service typically covers:

  • Escape route lighting plan
  • LED exit signs and emergency light fittings
  • Battery backup verification
  • Simulated power-failure testing
01
