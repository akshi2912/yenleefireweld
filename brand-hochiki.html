About Hochiki

Founded in Japan in 1918, Hochiki has spent over 100 years designing and manufacturing fire detection and emergency lighting equipment, with production facilities in Japan, the United Kingdom and the United States. Its analogue addressable and conventional detectors are tested to EN54, UL and FM standards and are specified in commercial and industrial buildings around the world for their long-term reliability and low false-alarm rates.
