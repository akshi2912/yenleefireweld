This fall arrest kit pairs an adjustable full-body harness with dorsal, sternal and hip attachment points to a shock-absorbing lanyard rated to reduce arrest forces during a fall. Padded shoulder and leg straps improve comfort during extended wear, and all hardware is rated to recognised work-at-height standards.

Harness TypeFull-body, adjustable, padded straps
Attachment PointsDorsal, sternal & hip D-rings
LanyardShock-absorbing, single or twin-leg options
BucklesQuick-connect, corrosion-resistant
Rated CapacityRated per ANSI/EN work-at-height standard
Size RangeAdjustable to fit most body types
StandardANSI Z359.11 & EN 361 compliant
MaintenancePre-use inspection & annual certification
