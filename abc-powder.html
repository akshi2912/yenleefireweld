The ABC Dry Powder Fire Extinguisher uses a mono-ammonium phosphate based agent that smothers the fire triangle by interrupting the chemical reaction and coating the fuel surface, preventing re-ignition. Its multi-class rating makes it the most flexible extinguisher on the market — a single unit can be relied upon across mixed-risk areas where paper, wood, fabric, fuel, oil, and gas hazards may all be present.

Every unit is pressure-tested at the factory, supplied with a tamper seal and pressure gauge for at-a-glance status checks, and comes with a wall bracket for compliant mounting. Yen Lee Fireweld backs every extinguisher sold with optional annual maintenance contracts to keep your premises compliant with SCDF fire safety regulations.
