Built for permit-required confined space entry, this kit centres on a rated aluminium tripod, a 4:1 mechanical-advantage winch/haul system and a Class III full-body harness with dorsal D-ring. It supports both non-entry retrieval and attended entry rescue, with a dedicated belay line for redundancy. Every component is rated, load-tested and supplied ready to deploy.

TripodRated aluminium, adjustable leg, 3-point base
Winch System4:1 mechanical-advantage haul, self-locking
HarnessClass III full-body, dorsal & sternal D-rings
RopeLow-stretch life-safety rope, main & belay lines
Rated LoadRated for one-person raise/lower per system spec
Bag/CaseRugged carry case with labelled component pockets
StandardOSHA 1910.146 & NFPA 1670 aligned
MaintenanceAnnual inspection & recertification recommended
  • Supports both non-entry retrieval and attended entry rescue
  • Self-locking 4:1 haul system for controlled raise/lower
  • Class III harness with dual attachment points
  • Independent main and belay line for built-in redundancy
  • Compact tripod footprint suited to tight access points
  • Compatible with our annual inspection & recertification programme
